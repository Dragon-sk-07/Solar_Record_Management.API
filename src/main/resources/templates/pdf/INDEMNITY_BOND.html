SOLAR PANELS
  1. Panels should be cleaned at least once every fifteen days.
  2. Bird droppings should be cleaned immediately.
  3. Use water and soft sponge / cloth.
  4. Do not use abrasive material.
CABLES AND CONNECTION BOXES
  1. Check tightness and corrosion.
  2. Check water seals.
  3. Check insulation damage.
INVERTER
  1. Install in clean dry ventilated area.
  2. Remove dust periodically.
  3. Check automatic disconnection.
SHUTTING DOWN THE SYSTEM
  1. Disconnect all sources.
  2. Cover modules before maintenance.
  3. Avoid shutdown during peak generation.
